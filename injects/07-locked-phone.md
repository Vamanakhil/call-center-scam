# Inject 07 — Locked Phone

**Time:** 12:22 IST
**Where:** Manager's pocket
**Card to read aloud:**

> "Constable pulls the manager's iPhone 14 Pro. It's locked with a 6-digit PIN. The manager refuses to give the PIN."

## What the trainee team should do
1. Photograph the phone ON, screen showing the lock screen.
2. Note model, IMEI (dial *#06#), SIM ICCID, phone number.
3. **Enable Airplane Mode** (swipe down → Airplane). DO NOT swipe to power off — may trigger wipe.
4. Place in **Faraday bag**, seal.
5. Note battery % and last charge time.
6. Do **NOT** try common PINs (123456, 000000) — burns the 5-attempt grace before the 10-attempt lockout.
7. Image via **Cellebrite UFED** within 4 hours (or chip-off if Cellebrite can't unlock).

## What they should find
- The iPhone 14 Pro has a 30-attempt limit before permanent lock. With Cellebrite Premium, you can sometimes unlock with 0 attempts; otherwise wait for the iPhone to age.
- The burner Android is unlocked (no PIN). Logical extraction via Cellebrite UFED yields Telegram chats, contacts, SMS.

## Common mistake
❌ Trying common PINs (123456, 000000). Burns attempts.

## Grading
- ✅ (5) Image via UFED, do not exceed 5 PIN attempts, bag in Faraday
- 🟡 (3) Try one or two common PINs
- ❌ (0) Brute-force the PIN
