# Inject 16 — CCTV DVR

**Time:** 13:00 IST
**Where:** Corner under a cloth
**Card:** "Constable finds a small CCTV DVR in the corner under a cloth."

## Response
1. **Photograph in situ**.
2. Pull the HDD (DVRs usually have a 2.5" SATA HDD).
3. Image the HDD with FTK Imager.
4. Check the recycle bin on the embedded Linux.

## Find
- DVR holds 14 days of footage.
- The recycle bin has 3 deleted .mp4 files from 16-Apr-2026 — recovered.

## Mistake
❌ Dismantling the DVR without imaging the HDD first.

## Grading
- ✅ Photograph, pull HDD, image, check recycle bin
- 🟡 Photograph only
- ❌ Dismantle without imaging
